Architecture

Built as six small engines.

Capture

Records consumed pages, posts, videos, searches, timestamps, and content evidence.

Inference

Turns captured activity into themes and meaning without guessing conclusions.

Schema

Detects repeated mental-frame signals as context, not diagnosis.

Origin

Finds direct source candidates with strong wording or phrase overlap.

Influence

Maps repeated shaping patterns across consumed activity.

Interface

Turns the evidence into a clear answer with citations the user can open.
